ABOUT US

We Are A Creative Event Management Company.

Naza Kings Events is the leading event management and planning company in Nigeria. Established in 2019, our goal has been to create bespoke exceptional events for our varied clientele. We believe every event has got a brand/reputation to protect and promote, that is why we pay the utmost attention to detail in all our undertakings.

We take pride in creating one-of-a-kind experiences — whether it’s an intimate gathering or a fabulous fête, we produce memorable and unique events of all shapes, sizes and styles with rentals of Canopies,Seats, Air conditional Tent,Cold Van, Standing Fan,Plates, Coolers,Spoons & Cathering,Decoration and Makeup Ushers,Life Band,DJ and Media Services.
